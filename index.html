JAM² Features

More mountains, more smiles. Keep scrolling to dive deep into the technology powering our most dynamic e-MTB yet.

E-Drive System

Bosch Performance Line CX

85 Nm of pure, dynamic power tailored for the steepest climbs and toughest trails.

The e-drive is perfectly integrated into the bottom bracket area. This lowers the center of gravity and heavily improves your ride stability. Thanks to the 85 Nm torque of the Bosch Performance Line CX, you can conquer any uphill obstacle.

Customizable via eBike Flow App

Using the smart system, you can customize your riding modes perfectly to your needs via the eBike Flow app. Tune the support, dynamics, maximum speed, and maximum torque of the eMTB and Tour+ modes to your individual riding style.

Energy & Range

750 Wh Integrated Battery

Endless laps on your favorite trails with unmatched battery capacity.

The battery is elegantly integrated into the down tube, yet it can be removed easily for charging if you don't want to bring your bike into the living room. The massive 750 Wh capacity means range anxiety is a thing of the past.

Smart Placement

To ensure the handling remains playful despite the larger battery, we placed it as low as possible. The weight distribution is finely balanced, giving you confidence on high-speed descents while keeping the front wheel planted on steep technical climbs.

Kinematics

Upgraded F.O.L.D. Kinematics

150mm of plush, progressive travel designed for heavy hits and active riding.

We've optimized our F.O.L.D. (FOCUS Optimized Linkage Design) kinematics for more progression. This means you can ride harder and faster without worrying about bottoming out, while still enjoying extreme sensitivity over small bumps.

Better Coil Compatibility

Because the kinematics are now much more progressive, you can easily install a coil shock if you prefer maximum traction over the air shock's adjustability. The lower standover height gives you more room to move on technical terrain.

Adaptability

Adjustable Geometry Flip Chip

Tailor your JAM² to perfectly match your riding style and local trails.

By simply turning the headset cups, you can easily change the head angle of your JAM² from a slack 65.5° for agile handling to an aggressive 64.5° for pure downhill focus.

Mullet Ready

Want to run a 27.5" rear wheel? No problem. The secondary FLIP CHIP at the rear linkage allows you to adjust the chainstay length, keeping the bottom bracket height perfect whether you're running a full 29er setup or a mixed wheel mullet configuration for maximum agility.
